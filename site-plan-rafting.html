Overview

Purpose

Our purpose here at Dry Oar is to offer the best experience for our friends to learn about the joy which is White Water Rafting. We plan to do this by doing the following:

1: We will educate our friends in how to particpate safely in this high adventure experience while enjoying it at its fullest.

2: We have dedicated ourselves to finding employees who want to make the experience you will have with us a unforgettable one. When you are with us you are family

3: Lastly, we want everyone to know that we care for these waters and want everyone to understand that nature is meant to be enjoyed.

Audience

We want everyone to experience the joys that come with white water rafting. We truly believe that both those young and old can have a safe and thrilling experience. Those of you that have the heart and desire to seek out high adventure have found the right place and we are commited to bringing that spirit to life. If you are seeking for more information on how to get started then you have come to the right place! Welcome to the great life and we are exited that you are here.
